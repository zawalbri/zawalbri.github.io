Introduction

Dozy Coffee

Dozy Coffee is a cafe located in San Diego, California. The cafe focuses on serving quality coffee, crafting specialty drinks, and creating a welcoming space for photographers and creatives through its vintage, film photography themed interior.

Target audience

The Dozy Coffee website is for those who want to visit and/or learn more about Dozy Coffee. The cafe's target audience is coffee enthusiasts, photographers, creatives, and anyone who wants a good quality coffee.

Users of the website will primarily want to browse the cafe's menu, find out store information such as location, hours, and contact information, as well as learn more about Dozy Coffee and its story.

About

[Cameras and coffees]

Dozy Coffee came about the way most cafes do: someone wanted to bring their delicious beverages to the world. The owner, Zoe, considers herself a coffee enthustiast and photographer — So, why not combine the two? Zoe wanted a place where photographers and creatives could enjoy fun drinks in a visually-inspiring place.

Supporing local creatives is important to us, so we sell photos, prints, and designs from local artists. In addition, you can stop by our shop to browse vintage and second-hand cameras to begin your own photography journey!
