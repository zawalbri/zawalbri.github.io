About Us

Dozy Coffee came about the way most cafes do: someone wanted to bring their delicious beverages to the world. The owner, Zoe, considers herself a coffee enthustiast and photographer — So, why not combine the two? Zoe wanted a place where photographers and creatives could enjoy fun drinks in a visually-inspiring environment.

Supporting local creatives is important to Dozy Coffee, so we sell photos, prints, and designs from local artists. In addition, you can stop by our shop to browse vintage and second-hand cameras to begin your own photography journey!
